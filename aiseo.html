Questions

The honest answers.

Will this guarantee that ChatGPT recommends my business?+
No responsible provider can guarantee that. What we do is improve the signals that make your business easier for Google and AI search tools to access, understand, verify and potentially recommend.
Is this just SEO with a new name?+
It overlaps with SEO, but the focus is different. Traditional SEO is mostly about rankings and traffic. AI visibility is about whether machines can clearly understand your business, trust the information and use it when answering buyer questions.
Why not just ask ChatGPT to create the files and fixes?+
The value is not simply creating files or copy. The value is knowing what to check, what matters, what to ignore, how to prioritise the issues and how to connect the technical, content and trust signals into one clear visibility foundation.
Do I need a full website rebuild?+
Usually not. Most businesses need their existing website and public information made clearer, more structured and easier to verify before they consider a rebuild.
Do I need to pay monthly for this to work?+
No. The sprint is designed as a standalone foundation fix. Monthly support is optional for businesses that want ongoing Google Business Profile activity, AI visibility checks, review support, content improvements and competitor monitoring.
Who is this best for?+
It is best for service businesses, local businesses, clinics, professional firms, trades, home improvement companies and B2B providers where trust, expertise and location matter before a buyer makes an enquiry.
What happens after the sprint?+
You receive a clear action plan. Some businesses stop there. Others continue with monthly Google and AI visibility monitoring, Google Business Profile support, content improvements or lead reactivation campaigns.
