Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions before you get started? Here are some of the most common inquiries we receive.

What results can we expect from SilverBullet Pro?

+

Clients typically see a significant reduction in missed high-value leads, faster client acquisition, and a more efficient triage process for all inbound voicemails. Our ROI calculator can help estimate your potential gains.

How long does it take to set everything up?

+

Our white-glove setup service typically gets you live within 3-5 business days. This includes number setup (if applicable), AI tuning, and integration with your existing systems.

Do you integrate with our existing VoIP/phone system?

+

Yes, for our "Solo Professional" and "Multi-Partner Firm" tiers, we integrate with most modern VoIP systems to seamlessly divert voicemails for AI triage.

Is SilverBullet Pro GDPR compliant?

+

Absolutely. We prioritize data privacy and security, offering full GDPR compliance (consent, access, erasure), data residency options, and comprehensive audit logs for transparent oversight.

How does the AI understand our specific business needs?

+

Our AI is not generic. During onboarding, we tune it using specific examples and language relevant to your industry and firm, ensuring it accurately identifies urgency, client value, and key instructions.

What if we don't have a VoIP system?

+

Our "Complete" plan is designed for you! We provide a dedicated Twilio phone number that we configure for your business, seamlessly connecting it with SilverBullet Pro for an all-in-one solution.

Can we try SilverBullet Pro before committing?

+

We do not offer a free trial, but we provide a comprehensive, no-obligation demo that showcases exactly how SilverBullet Pro will integrate with your operations and deliver value.

What kind of support is included?

+

All plans include dedicated support, with priority levels and SLAs increasing for our "Multi-Partner Firm" and "Enterprise" clients. You'll always have access to our expert team.
