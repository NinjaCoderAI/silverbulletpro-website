FAQ

Questions We
Actually Get Asked.

Is this GDPR compliant?

Yes. We operate under Legitimate Interest provisions in UK GDPR and PECR — the same legal basis used by every major B2C sales team. We only contact individuals your business has already interacted with. Opt-outs are processed automatically and instantly via our suppression system. We'll also ask you to sign a simple declaration confirming the data is yours before we begin.

Do I need to install anything?

Nothing. Export your contacts as a CSV, fill in a short onboarding form telling us your offer, and we handle everything from there — data cleaning, AI setup, deployment, and lead delivery. Your only job is to answer the phone when hot leads come in.

What counts as a "qualified lead"?

A contact who replies positively to our outreach and either confirms availability for a callback or books via your calendar link. We're not counting curious replies or accidental responses — these are people who have expressed genuine interest in hearing from you.

What if my data is old or messy?

We expect it. Our team cleanses and formats every database before deployment. Even data from 2–3 years ago typically yields 3–8% engagement when messaged correctly. The AI adjusts its approach based on data recency.

How does the No Win, No Fee guarantee work?

Simple. If we don't deliver the guaranteed minimum number of qualified leads for your tier by the end of the campaign, we refund you in full. No questions, no small print. We're only in the business of getting results.
