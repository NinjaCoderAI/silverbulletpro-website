Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to change my phone number?

+

No. You keep your existing business number that your customers already know and trust. Our system works by simply processing the voicemail notifications that your phone provider already sends to your email.

How does it connect to my phone?

+

SilverBullet Swift connects to your phone's existing "voicemail-to-email" feature. Most modern providers (like O2, Vodafone, RingCentral, etc.) can send you an email with an MP3 audio file of a voicemail. You simply set up an automatic forwarding rule in your email to send those specific emails to us, and our AI handles the rest.

Is it hard to set up?

+

Not at all. If you can set up an email filter or forwarding rule (e.g., "forward all emails from 'voicemail@myprovider.com'"), you can set up SilverBullet Swift. We provide simple, step-by-step instructions after you sign up.
