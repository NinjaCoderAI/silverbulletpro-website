The Technical Reason You Are Blocked

If your website is failing to capture AI recommendations, it is almost certainly due to one of these two technical flaws:

  • 01
    The robots.txt Blackout

    Your website's instruction manual (robots.txt) explicitly disallows AI user-agents like GPTBot or ClaudeBot. It's like putting a digital padlock on your front door. The AI simply turns around and recommends your competitor.

  • 02
    Missing the llms.txt File

    AI engines hate reading messy HTML websites. In late 2024, a new standard called `llms.txt` was introduced. It acts as a clean, Markdown roadmap specifically for AI. If your site returns a 404 error for this file, the AI has to guess what you do—and it usually guesses wrong.
